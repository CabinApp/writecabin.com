Principles

01

Writing comes first.

Planning, references and worldbuilding orbit the manuscript. They never replace it.

02

Complexity is earned.

Cabin begins quiet. Structure appears only when the project is ready for it.

03

Your work belongs to you.

Local-first files, readable folders and clean exports keep the writer in control.

04

Calm before clever.

The interface should feel like a prepared room, not a system asking to be managed.

05

AI assists; it does not author.

AI may notice grammar, pacing or continuity concerns. The voice remains human.

Current status

Cabin is being built slowly and deliberately.
