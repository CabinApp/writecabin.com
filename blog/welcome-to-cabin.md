## Hi! Welcome to Cabin!

Cabin is writing software that is designed to grow with the writer. Arising from my personal frustration of different existing writing programs not supporting some or the other feature, I decided to use my software development knowledge to create an app that grows with the writer.

## The Purpose of this Blog

I have only recently conceptualised and started work on Cabin. This blog will be a running collection of insights, design choices, research findings, and devlogs. You can gain an insight into my process and track the progress of the project on a much finer scale than the [Roadmap](https://writecabin.com/roadmap.html)
